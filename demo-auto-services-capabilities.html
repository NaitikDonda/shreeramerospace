Capabilities

World-Class Manufacturing Excellence

Ultra High Precision Machining

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced multi-axis CNC machines capable of ultra-precision machining for complex aerospace components. We specialize in machining critical parts with tight tolerances and complex geometries.

  • Multi-axis machining centers with 5-axis capability
  • Tolerances up to ±0.005mm for critical components
  • Capability to machine from few grams to 9000 KG
  • Thermally stabilized machining environment
  • Advanced tooling with shrink-fit technology

Prototype Development

We offer comprehensive prototype development services from concept to production. Our team works closely with clients to develop prototypes that meet exact specifications and performance requirements.

  • Rapid prototyping with quick turnaround times
  • CAD/CAM support with CATIA and NX software
  • Design for manufacturability analysis
  • Iterative development and testing
  • Seamless transition to production

Quality Inspection

Our quality control facility is equipped with advanced inspection systems to ensure every component meets the highest standards. We have in-house capability for comprehensive inspection and verification.

  • 3-D CMM from Hexagon (4000 × 1800 mm bed size)
  • 2-D Height masters from Timos Electronic
  • Complete range of manual inspection instruments
  • Online machine probing system
  • In-house machine calibration system

Fabrication

Our fabrication capabilities include complete assembly and integration services. We have dedicated assembly areas with controlled environment facilities for critical components.

  • In-house Jigs and Fixtures development
  • Clean assembly section (17,000 Sq. Ft.)
  • Controlled environment assembly
  • Complete sub-assembly capabilities
  • Stress relieving and painting services
