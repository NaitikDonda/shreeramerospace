Company Overview

With a strong base of technical excellence and the working philosophy of "Rising Together", SHREERAM AEROSPACE AND DEFENCE LLP operates at the upper end of the technology spectrum and has been at the forefront of providing value-added solutions in ultra-high precision and critical metal cutting for sophisticated and high-tech industrial sectors.

The company envisions establishing a state-of-the-art metal cutting unit capable of directly supplying aerospace majors such as ISRO, HAL, Boeing, Airbus, and Rolls-Royce. Management established Shreeram Aerospace & Defence LLP in 2017 with a vision of becoming a key aerospace manufacturing partner.

18+ Capital Equipment

Advanced machinery worth 10 million USD

5,000 sq. meters

Constructed facility area

Before entering the aerospace sector, the company invested its first 4 years strengthening internal capabilities and clearing audits of major aerospace organizations. Within less than 3 years, the company delivered 600+ aerospace components and toolings.
