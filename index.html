About Shreeram Aerospace

Established in 2017, Shreeram Aerospace & Defence LLP is a leading precision manufacturing company delivering high-quality, reliable and innovative solutions for aerospace, defence and high-tech industries worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art facility and skilled workforce enable us to deliver complex components with exceptional precision and quality, meeting the stringent requirements of the aerospace industry.

Our Services

What We Do

Total end-to-end manufacturing solutions — from raw material to final assembly

Step 01

Raw Material Sourcing

100% traceability, AS9100 certified vendors, bar stocks, castings and forgings in aerospace-grade alloys.

Step 02

Structure Fabrication

Cutting, welding, NDT, stress relieving, machining and painting for structural aerospace components.

Step 03

Ultra Precision Machining

Multi-axis machining up to 3500 × 2000 mm with aerospace-grade tolerances as tight as 6 microns.

Step 04

Assembly & Testing

Large CMM inspection, controlled assembly areas and advanced quality systems for verified, flight-ready components.
